About Us

We Are Arocom

Arocom is a consulting and solution engineering company in niche skills like Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Cloud Computing. Our clients include companies within drug discovery, therapeutic medicine, heavy machinery and startups. Our consultants are best in their skills and offer hands-on talent to our clients in achieving their goals.

Focused on the evolving need of the customer, Arocom IT Solutions was founded in 2014 with eCoursewiz as an online training portal. Over the years it has turned into a consulting organization focused on Artificial Intelligence, data science, and cloud computing specializing in domains like life sciences, heavy machinery, and retail.

The company today offers solutions in the area of data science and helps them to smoothly execute the disruption curve strongly believing that data is the new fuel for economic growth. The team of professionals at AROCOM has worked with world-leading organizations from areas of Research & Development, Academics, and fortune 500 companies.
